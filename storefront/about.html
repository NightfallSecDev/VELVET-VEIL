Velvet & Veil was born from a singular, uncompromising vision: to bridge the gap between high-performance editorial makeup and skin-nourishing clean botanical science.

The Editorial Genesis

Redefining the Architecture of Modern Beauty

Founded between the haute couture runways of Paris and the vibrant, bespoke artisan labs of Mumbai, Velvet & Veil challenged the traditional cosmetic paradigm. For decades, luxury beauty forced a compromise: endure heavy, pore-clogging synthetic waxes to achieve intense pigment, or accept sheer, fleeting color in the name of clean skincare.

Our proprietary micro-milling process shatters this dichotomy. By calibrating pure mineral pigments down to 0.1 microns and suspending them in cold-pressed organic Argan oil and plant-derived squalane, we created formulas that deliver breathtaking, one-swipe opacity while actively repairing the skin barrier.

"True luxury is absolute transparency. It is knowing that what touches your skin is as pristine as it is high-performance."

Today, Velvet & Veil is the signature choice of lead runway artists, beauty editors, and discerning VIP clients worldwide who demand nothing less than absolute perfection.
