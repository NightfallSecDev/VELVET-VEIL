Refund & Exchange Policy

Last Updated: May 18, 2026

1. The Velvet & Guarantee

We stand behind the pristine quality and rich pigment of our formulations. If you are not entirely satisfied with your purchase, or if the skin-matching tone is not an absolute perfect fit, we are here to help with our complimentary 30-day concierge exchange and refund policy.

2. Complimentary Shade Exchanges

Finding the perfect foundation or lip serum shade online can be challenging. We offer complimentary shade exchanges within 30 days of delivery. Simply reach out to our concierge team with your order number, and we will ship your replacement shade immediately upon receipt of the original item.

3. Eligibility for Refunds

To be eligible for a full refund, your item must be unused, in the same premium condition that you received it, and in its original luxury packaging. Once your return is received and inspected by our flagship lab, we will send you an email notification confirming the approval or rejection of your refund.

4. Processing Refunds

If approved, your refund will be processed immediately through our secure client gateway, and a credit will automatically be applied to your credit card or original method of payment within 3 to 5 business days.
