Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 18, 2026

1. Introduction & Premium Commitment

At Velvet & Veil, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy informs you about how we look after your personal data when you visit our storefront and tells you about your privacy rights and how the law protects you.

2. Data Security & Client Storage Architecture

All customer transactions, custom shade selections, and account details are securely encrypted and stored within our dedicated client storage architecture. We utilize advanced transport layer security (TLS) to ensure that your sensitive payment and shipping information remains strictly confidential.

3. Information We Collect

We may collect, use, store, and transfer different kinds of personal data about you, including Identity Data (first name, last name), Contact Data (billing address, delivery address, email address, telephone numbers), and Transaction Data (details about payments to and from you, including selected makeup shade variants and order items).

4. Your Legal Rights

Under data protection laws, you have rights including the right to request access, correction, erasure, or restriction of your personal data. To exercise any of these rights, please contact our VIP concierge team at concierge@velvetandveil.com.
